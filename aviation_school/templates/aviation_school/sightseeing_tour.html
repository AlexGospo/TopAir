City Tour

People from here or elsewhere, Top Air offers you an overview of Montreal. A breathtaking flight awaits you at an altitude of some 3,000 feet. You will observe the different municipalities of the region as well as the Mont Royal and its magnificent landscape. * Maximum of three people per flight.

Valley Tour

Explore the beauty of nature from a new perspective with a flight tour over breathtaking landscapes. Soar through the sky and admire panoramic views of towering mountains, rolling hills, and sparkling lakes. Get up close and personal with towering waterfalls and watch as they cascade down the mountainside. Take in the endless stretch of rolling green fields dotted with quaint towns and villages.

Fly over vibrant forests, dotted with colorful leaves and teeming with wildlife. Experience the wonder of flight and marvel at the beauty of the natural world with a flight tour over some of the world's most awe-inspiring landscapes.

Pilot For 1 Day

From takeoff to landing, take control of the aircraft yourself accompanied by an experienced instructor. In a safe way, you can experience basic and advanced maneuvers to meet your expectations.

This first introductory flight can be credited to you if the passion leads you to start your training with us. After approximately 2 hours of flight, you will receive your certificate of participation. Take to the skies and discover the region seen from the sky!

Aerobatic Flight
